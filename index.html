About me

I first starting 'coding' in 1998, but I didn't consider myself a 'coder'; I was "putting tags around text". Companies having their own websites was a new thing back then, and whilst working for a legal publications company.

I didn't really understand the technical intricacies of what I was doing, but I really enjoyed it. Especially having to look for 'broken' code when text did not display properly.

I have always been drawn to projects and tasks that require problem solving as well as creativity.
